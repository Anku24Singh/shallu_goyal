About Me

We learn what we see, right?

Growing up, I watched my father lead with kindness, patience, and empathy — and I carry those values with me every day. If there's one thing people often remember me by, it's my smile. I believe in sharing happiness and multiplying it — that's my soul goal.

Before founding this platform, I worked at a private sector bank for four years. During that time, I discovered something powerful — my ability to genuinely connect with people, uplift them, and bring joy to their lives just by being present, listening, and caring. That’s when I realised my true purpose: to help people lead happier, more emotionally fulfilling lives.

Our education system teaches us so much, but rarely how to understand or manage our emotions. We’re never taught how to handle stress, anxiety, or sadness — yet these emotions have become part of our daily lives. That’s why I created this space — to help people reconnect with themselves, cope with stress, and cultivate genuine happiness from within.

Today, through my workshops, one-on-one sessions, and wellness programs, I’m grateful to be making a real impact. I’ve worked with young professionals facing burnout, homemakers rediscovering their spark, and even corporate teams looking for more balance and joy. Seeing people shift their mindset, regain their energy, and choose happiness — that’s what keeps me going.

With mindfulness, self-care, and simple mindset shifts, I believe each of us can create a life that doesn’t just look good on the outside, but feels deeply joyful and aligned on the inside. I’m here to walk that journey with you.

These simple yet powerful tools are here to support your journey toward greater self-awareness and emotional clarity.

A Note from the Founder

Have you ever wondered why the kids who were carefree, curious, and full of wonder — not necessarily the toppers — often grow up to lead more successful, fulfilling lives? They weren’t always chasing marks or approval. They questioned the rules, followed their joy, and stayed connected to themselves.

We didn’t notice it back then — but growing up, we saw it unfold.

That simple truth has stayed with me. We thrive not by ticking boxes, but by living freely, mindfully, and with awareness of our inner world. Through my work as a wellness and happiness coach, I help you reconnect with that freedom — the spark you once had — and guide you through the mindset shifts that lead to a life that feels deeply aligned, joyful, and whole.
